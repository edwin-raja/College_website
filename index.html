Course We Offer

We are offer to three best computer science course for student by our University

CSE

The CSE course is a full-time four-year (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and Engineering. This degree provides students with a comprehensive foundation in computer science, software engineering, and the principles of computing. The program covers a wide range of topics including algorithms, data structures, programming languages, software development, computer networks, artificial intelligence, and more. It prepares students for a dynamic career in technology, equipping them with the skills needed to excel in fields such as software development, data science, cybersecurity, and machine learning.

IT

The IT course is a full-time four-year (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology. This program focuses on the application of technology to solve business and organizational problems. It covers a broad range of topics including programming, database management, network administration, cybersecurity, web development, and information systems. The degree equips students with the skills to design, implement, and manage technology solutions in various industries, preparing them for roles such as IT consultant, systems analyst, network engineer, and database administrator.

AIML

The AIML course is a full-time four-year (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This program focuses on the design and development of intelligent systems and machine learning models. It covers a wide range of topics including algorithms, data analysis, neural networks, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics. Students gain practical experience in developing AI solutions and applying machine learning techniques to real-world problems. The degree prepares graduates for careers in AI research, data science, software development, and more.
